03 · THE FORMAT
The event · one court, one afternoon, one point

One Point vs. The Legend.

Run of show · ~3 hours
  • The Arrival — legend walks on, deadpan: "I've beaten Grand Slam players. Today, a CFO."
  • The Gauntlet — everyone gets one point against the pro. Employees, clients, friends. 60–90 seconds per person, queue keeps moving.
  • The Tie — mini team match: challengers drafted into Blue vs Red squads, legend plays for both. Trophy for the winning side.
  • The Prize — win your point: £100 FX credit (FCA sign-off; venue-only mechanic). Everyone: instant personal clip via QR.
Why it works
  • Democratic — a 60-year-old client and a 24-year-old analyst have the same shot. One point is winnable enough to be irresistible.
  • Fast content — every point is a self-contained 20-second clip with a natural arc: hope → destruction (or glory).
  • Client-friendly — invite-only for customers = compliant hospitality, no public ticket promotion needed.
  • Repeatable — works as an annual property. Year two books itself.

This cast powers three weeks of content before a single ball is hit at the O2 — and every asset is a WorldFirst asset, forever.
